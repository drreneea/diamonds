Haniken Jewelers

Haniken Jewelers is a family-owned and operated business that has been providing exquisite diamond jewelry since its establishment in 1985. With a keen focus on offering high-quality diamonds, they have built a solid reputation in the jewelry industry, serving customers not just in their New York location, but around the world. They are known for their personalized service and their commitment to helping clients find the perfect piece of jewelry to fit their taste and budget.

At Haniken Jewelers, customers can expect a wide range of diamond jewelry options. They specialize in offering a variety of diamond rings, earrings, bracelets, and necklaces. The company prides itself on providing diamonds that are ethically sourced and certified, ensuring customers receive not only beautiful jewelry but also peace of mind. Whether it's for a special occasion or a personal treat, Haniken Jewelers has the expertise and the collection to help customers find their ideal diamond piece.
