Menashe & Sons Jewelers

Menashe & Sons Jewelers is a family-owned business located in West Seattle, Washington that offers an exquisite collection of diamond jewelry. With over 40 years of experience in the jewelry industry, they are renowned for their exceptional quality, craftsmanship, and personalized customer service. Their vast selection includes diamond engagement rings, wedding bands, earrings, necklaces, and custom pieces, all meticulously crafted to meet their customers' high standards.

Their team of expert jewelers also offer professional jewelry repair, restoration, and cleaning services, adding further convenience for their customers. Menashe & Sons Jewelers also provides complimentary appraisals and consultations, ensuring customers receive not only beautiful jewelry but also a comprehensive understanding of their purchase. Their commitment to quality, customer service, and integrity has made them a trusted choice for those seeking exceptional diamond jewelry in the Seattle area.
