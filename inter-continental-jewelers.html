Inter-Continental Jewelers

Inter-Continental Jewelers is a Houston-based diamond jewelry store with a rich history dating back to 1978. Founded by Suzanne Grover, this family-owned operation boasts an extensive selection of diamond jewelry, including engagement rings, wedding bands, and custom pieces. With a keen focus on customer satisfaction, Inter-Continental Jewelers is deeply committed to providing high quality, beautifully crafted diamond jewelry that brings joy and sparkle to life's precious moments.

Their expertise extends beyond standard jewelry sales as they also offer services like jewelry repair and diamond upgrade programs. They use GIA certified diamonds, ensuring that their customers get only the best.
