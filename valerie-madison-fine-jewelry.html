Valerie Madison Fine Jewelry

Valerie Madison Fine Jewelry is a Seattle-based boutique that crafts exceptional diamond jewelry. Founded by Valerie Madison, the company is dedicated to creating beautiful pieces that incorporate her love for minimalist and elegant designs. With a background in environmental science, Valerie aims to minimize the environmental impact of her business by using primarily recycled metals, ethically sourced diamonds, and moissanites in her designs.

The business stands out for its commitment to sustainability and its unique approach to designing diamond jewelry that's both modern and timeless. Valerie Madison Fine Jewelry offers a broad range of pieces, from engagement rings and wedding bands to everyday wear. Each piece is meticulously designed and handcrafted by Valerie and her small team, ensuring personalized attention and high-quality results for each item they create.
