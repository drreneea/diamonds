L. Priori Jewelry

L. Priori Jewelry is a Philadelphia-based jewelry store dedicated to offering an exquisite range of diamond jewelry. Founded by Lauren Priori, a GIA Graduate Gemologist, the store has built its reputation on its commitment to quality, craftsmanship, and personalized service. With a keen eye for detail, Lauren creates custom pieces that are a beautiful blend of classic aesthetics and modern sensibility, catering to those in search of elegant and timeless jewelry pieces.

L. Priori Jewelry offers a wide range of services including custom design, restoration of vintage pieces, and private jewelry consultations. The store prides itself on its bespoke service, where clients can collaborate with Lauren to create unique, one-of-a-kind pieces. With an emphasis on education, L. Priori Jewelry ensures clients are well-informed about their purchases, providing expertise on diamonds, gemstones, and precious metals. With L. Priori Jewelry, customers can expect a personal and enjoyable jewelry shopping experience.
