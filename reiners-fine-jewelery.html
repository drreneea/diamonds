Reiner's Fine Jewelery

Reiner's Fine Jewelry, based in Houston, Texas, is a family-owned business that has been serving the community with exceptional diamond jewelry for over three generations. Established in 1913, the company prides itself on its rich history and commitment to providing customers with high-quality, beautiful diamond pieces. With a vast selection of jewelry, from engagement rings to custom designs, every piece reflects the craftsmanship, expertise and dedication that Reiner's Fine Jewelry stands for.

The core value of Reiner's Fine Jewelry is to build trust with their customers by ensuring complete transparency in their services. They offer GIA-graded diamonds and provide expert guidance to customers throughout their shopping experience. Their knowledgeable staff helps clients understand the intricacies of diamonds, enabling them to make an informed decision. Reiner's Fine Jewelry is not just a store, it's a tradition of offering exceptional service and superior quality diamond jewelry.
