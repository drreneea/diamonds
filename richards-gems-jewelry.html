Richard's Gems & Jewelry

Since 1984, Richard's Gems & Jewelry has been a trusted source for fine diamonds and exquisite jewelry in Miami, Florida. Located in the famed Seybold Building, this family-owned and operated business prides itself on providing customers with high-quality products and personalized service. They specialize in diamonds, offering a wide selection from loose stones to beautifully crafted diamond jewelry, ensuring a perfect piece for every taste and occasion.

In addition to diamonds, Richard's Gems & Jewelry also offers a broad range of other precious gemstones, gold, and platinum jewelry, as well as timepieces. With a team of experienced jewelers on board, they provide expert repair and restoration services, and also offer custom design services for those seeking a unique piece. Whether you're shopping for an engagement ring, looking for a special gift, or simply want to treat yourself, Richard's Gems & Jewelry is committed to helping you find exactly what you're looking for.
