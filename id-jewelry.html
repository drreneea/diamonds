I.D. Jewelry

I.D. Jewelry, established in 1986, is a reputable diamond jeweler based in New York City's iconic Diamond District. Founded by Yekutiel Davidov, the family-operated business is renowned for their commitment to craftsmanship, quality, and customer service. They offer a wide range of diamond jewelry, including engagement rings, wedding bands, earrings, necklaces, and bracelets, each designed with a keen attention to detail.

I.D. Jewelry differentiates itself by offering a personalized shopping experience. They understand that buying diamond jewelry is often associated with significant life events and, therefore, strive to make every customer feel valued and satisfied. Their team of experienced professionals guide customers throughout the buying process, ensuring they make an informed decision. With a vast selection of GIA and EGL certified diamonds, I.D. Jewelry offers pieces that cater to a wide range of tastes and budgets.
