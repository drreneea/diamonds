Diamonds Direct

Diamonds Direct is a distinguished jewelry retailer with a primary focus on diamonds. Founded in 1995, the company's mission is to provide customers with high-quality diamond jewelry at a reasonable price point. Their business model, which eliminates the middlemen, allows them to deliver exceptional value to their customers, offering wholesale diamond prices directly to the public.

The company prides itself on its comprehensive selection of diamond jewelry, including engagement rings, wedding bands, and other fine jewelry. Diamonds Direct is renowned for its commitment to customer service, providing personalized consultation and guidance to help customers make informed decisions. With a network of stores across the United States, Diamonds Direct continues to expand its reach, bringing its unique approach to diamond retail to more customers nationwide.
