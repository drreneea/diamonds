Ofer Mizrahi Diamonds

Ofer Mizrahi Diamonds is a well-established diamond retailer with over three decades of experience in the industry. The company, with its headquarters based in Miami, has a global reach with additional offices located in New York, Hong Kong, and Israel. They specialize in natural fancy colored diamonds, offering an extensive range of colors, shapes, and sizes, designed to cater to a vast array of customer preferences.

The company's distinguishing factor lies in its commitment to ethical sourcing and sustainability. Ofer Mizrahi Diamonds ensures that all their diamonds are conflict-free and comply with the United Nations Kimberley Process. Their mission is to provide their customers with high-quality diamonds while maintaining a strong commitment to responsible sourcing. This makes them an excellent choice for customers looking for ethically sourced, beautiful diamonds.
