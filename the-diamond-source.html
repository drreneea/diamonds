The Diamond Source

The Diamond Source, based in San Diego, California, is a renowned jewelry store with a specialization in high-quality diamonds. Founded in 1989 by Maurice and Lisa Pitesky, this family-owned business has since been committed to providing customers with an extensive selection of diamonds for every occasion. The Diamond Source prides itself on its expertise, transparent pricing, and personalized service that ensure each customer's experience is tailored to their individual needs.

With over 30 years in the diamond industry, The Diamond Source has built a reputation for its meticulous selection of diamonds, all of which have been carefully examined and handpicked by Maurice Pitesky himself, a certified gemologist. The company guarantees the authenticity of their diamonds by providing GIA certification, the gold standard in the industry. Whether customers are seeking engagement rings, wedding bands, or other diamond jewelry, The Diamond Source provides a trusted and knowledgeable service to help them find the perfect piece.
