Maurice's Jewelers

Maurice's Jewelers is a reputable family-owned jewelry store that has been in operation for over four generations. Since its establishment, the store has been dedicated to offering a broad range of high-quality diamond jewelry. They have a commitment to excellence, ensuring they provide their customers with superior products and exceptional customer service. Based in Miami, Florida, Maurice's Jewelers also offers online shopping, making it accessible to customers nationwide.

In addition to diamond jewelry, Maurice's Jewelers provides a variety of other services including custom jewelry design, jewelry repair, and appraisal services. The store is recognized for its vast selection of engagement rings and wedding bands, as well as earrings, bracelets, and necklaces adorned with stunning diamonds. With a team of knowledgeable and passionate professionals, Maurice's Jewelers is dedicated to helping customers find the perfect piece of jewelry to celebrate their special moments in life.
