Shaw's Jewelry

Shaw's Jewelry has been a trusted name in the diamond jewelry industry for over 50 years. Established in 1966 in Houston, Texas, this family-owned business prides itself on its commitment to quality and customer service. The store offers a wide array of exquisite diamond jewelry, including engagement rings, bracelets, earrings, and pendants. Shaw's Jewelry is renowned for its expert knowledge and guidance in helping customers find the perfect piece to suit their individual needs and preferences.

The team at Shaw's Jewelry believes in building long-lasting relationships with its customers through honesty and integrity. Their expert jewelers take the time to educate customers about the different grades of diamonds, helping them make informed decisions. In addition to selling high-quality diamond jewelry, Shaw's Jewelry also offers repair and appraisal services, ensuring that every customer's jewelry needs are met under one roof.
