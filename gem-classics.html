Gem Classics

Gem Classics is a well-established jewelry store that has been offering an exceptional collection of diamond jewelry since 1983. Priding themselves on their commitment to quality and craftsmanship, they provide a range of diamond pieces from rings to bracelets, earrings, and necklaces. Each piece is designed with a keen eye for detail, reflecting the timeless beauty and elegance of diamonds.

Located in Addison, Texas, Gem Classics continues to serve customers with a personalized touch. They value customer satisfaction above all, striving to deliver a shopping experience that's both intimate and luxurious. Offering custom designs and repair services as well, Gem Classics is a trusted name in the world of diamond jewelry, committed to helping customers find the perfect piece for every occasion.
