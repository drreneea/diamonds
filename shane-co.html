Shane Co.

Shane Co. is a renowned family-owned jeweler with a longstanding reputation for offering a wide selection of ethically sourced diamonds, gemstones, and fine jewelry. The company, founded in 1971 by Tom Shane, is currently run by his son, Rordan Shane. It is best known for its direct diamond importation from sources around the world, which allows them to offer high-quality pieces at competitive prices.

Dedicated to transparency and customer satisfaction, Shane Co. provides a non-commission sales environment, meaning their team is focused on helping customers find the perfect piece rather than making a sale. Their lifetime warranty, free lifetime cleaning and maintenance services, and free 2-day shipping are testaments to their commitment to providing exceptional customer service. They offer a diverse range of jewelry styles and designs, ensuring customers can find pieces that align with their personal taste and budget.
