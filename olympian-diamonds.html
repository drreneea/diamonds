Olympian Diamonds

Olympian Diamonds is a family-run business that brings over 60 years of experience and expertise in the diamond industry. This Chicago-based company prides itself on sourcing and supplying diamonds of the highest quality, with a focus on ethical sourcing and transparency. Their extensive selection offers a wide range of options, including GIA-certified diamonds, for customers seeking exceptional stones for their jewelry.

With a dedication to providing excellent customer service, Olympian Diamonds aims to build lasting relationships with their clients. Their knowledgeable team offers personalized and professional service, guiding customers through the selection process to ensure they find the perfect diamond. Whether you're a seasoned gemologist or a first-time buyer, Olympian Diamonds offers an unrivaled level of experience and a vast selection of high-quality diamonds.
