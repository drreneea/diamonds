Zadok Jewelers

Zadok Jewelers is a prestigious family-owned business based in Houston, Texas, that has been offering a vast selection of exquisite diamond jewelry for over 45 years. Founded by Dror Zadok and his wife Helene, the company prides itself on the exceptional quality of its diamonds, all of which are handpicked by the Zadoks themselves. They offer a range of jewelry including engagement rings, wedding bands, necklaces, bracelets, and earrings, each piece reflecting a unique blend of classic elegance and modern design.

Zadok Jewelers is not just about selling jewelry; it's about creating lasting relationships with its customers. The company offers a personalized shopping experience, providing expert advice, custom design services, and jewelry repair. They are known for their impeccable customer service, ensuring every client feels valued and satisfied. Whether you're searching for the perfect engagement ring or a stunning necklace to mark a special occasion, Zadok Jewelers promises a shopping experience as unique and special as the piece you're looking for.
