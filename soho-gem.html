Soho Gem

Soho Gem is a high-end jewelry store situated in the heart of New York City. Established in 1998, the family-owned business has built a reputation for its unwavering commitment to offering a vast selection of finely crafted diamond and gemstone jewelry. Their specialty lies in handcrafting unique pieces, and they are particularly known for their exquisite range of engagement rings and wedding bands featuring ethically sourced, natural diamonds and precious stones.

Soho Gem is dedicated to providing a personalized shopping experience, making it an ideal destination for those seeking a blend of traditional and contemporary designs. Their team of experienced gemologists and jewelry experts guide customers through the process of choosing the perfect piece, ensuring each customer's needs are catered to. Whether you're searching for a custom design or one of their exclusive pieces, Soho Gem's commitment to quality and attention to detail ensures an unforgettable jewelry shopping experience.
