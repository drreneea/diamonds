Jae's Jewelers

Jae's Jewelers is a family-owned establishment with deep roots in the jewelry industry, having served customers since 1945. Based in Coral Gables, Florida, the store is recognized for its enduring commitment to quality and customer service, offering a wide selection of diamond jewelry. They specialize in GIA certified diamonds, designer jewelry, Swiss timepieces, and fine crystal gifts, alongside providing professional jewelry repair and watch repair services.

Jae's Jewelers takes pride in their ability to offer personalized service to each customer, ensuring a unique and satisfying shopping experience. The store also provides custom design services, allowing customers to create their own unique pieces of jewelry. With decades of experience and a reputation for integrity and professionalism, Jae's Jewelers continues to be a trusted name in the diamond jewelry business.
