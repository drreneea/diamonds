AV Diamonds

AV Diamonds is a Houston-based diamond and jewelry retailer that is dedicated to offering its customers an exceptional range of high-quality diamond products. Since its establishment in 1998, the company has built a strong reputation in the diamond industry for its unrelenting commitment to excellence, integrity, and customer satisfaction. AV Diamonds' extensive collection of diamond jewelry ranges from engagement and wedding rings to necklaces, bracelets, and earrings, each piece meticulously crafted to meet the diverse tastes and preferences of their clientele.

The company's expertise extends beyond just selling diamonds. AV Diamonds also provides comprehensive services which include custom jewelry design, jewelry repair, and appraisal services. Whether you're looking for a timeless piece to commemorate a special occasion or a unique design that reflects your personal style, AV Diamonds is equipped with the knowledge and skill to provide you with a piece that you will cherish for a lifetime. The company's commitment to quality and customer service has earned it the trust and loyalty of a vast clientele.
