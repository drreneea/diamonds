Design Jewelers

Design Jewelers is a well-established fine jewelry store based in San Francisco, specializing in diamond jewelry. With over four decades in the industry, they take pride in their expertise in creating custom designs and remodeling heirlooms, offering a personalized experience to their clients. Their collection ranges from beautifully crafted diamond rings, earrings, pendants to timeless wedding bands and engagement rings.

Bearing a strong commitment to quality, Design Jewelers sources their diamonds from reputable suppliers, ensuring that each piece is not only visually stunning but also ethically sourced. Their team of skilled artisans and knowledgeable staff works diligently to assist customers in finding or creating the perfect piece of jewelry that suits their style and budget. With Design Jewelers, customers can expect a seamless journey in finding their dream jewelry.
