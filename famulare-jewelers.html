Famulare Jewelers

Famulare Jewelers is a renowned jewelry store specializing in the sale of exquisite diamond pieces. Established in 1977, the family-owned business operates out of Carlsbad, California, with a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Their expertise in the industry is demonstrated through a diverse collection of carefully curated diamonds and other precious gemstones, each piece reflecting the mastery of craftsmanship and a keen eye for detail.

Famulare Jewelers is not just about selling jewelry; they also provide education about diamonds to their customers, helping them make informed decisions. The business offers a wide range of services including custom design, jewelry repair, and appraisals. With a focus on ethical sourcing, Famulare Jewelers ensures that all their diamonds are conflict-free, aligning their operations with their commitment to social responsibility.
