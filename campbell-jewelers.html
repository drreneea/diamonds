Campbell Jewelers

Campbell Jewelers, founded in 1948, is a trusted name in the diamond jewelry industry, committed to providing high-quality pieces to its customers. The company is a third-generation family-owned business, based in Memphis, Tennessee. It has maintained its legacy of professionalism and integrity by offering a diverse selection of fine jewelry, including diamond rings, pendants, earrings, and bracelets.

At Campbell Jewelers, clients can expect personalized service from experienced staff, ensuring a satisfying shopping experience. The company prioritizes customer satisfaction, offering custom design services to meet individual preferences and style. Over the years, Campbell Jewelers has earned a reputation for its exquisite craftsmanship, quality diamonds, and attentive service, making it a preferred choice for diamond jewelry buyers.
