Aura Diamonds

Aura Diamonds is a family-owned diamond wholesaler and custom diamond jeweler located in Dallas, Texas. With a focus on custom engagement rings and wedding bands, they have been helping clients find the perfect diamonds for their special moments for over 20 years. Their deep industry connections enable them to offer a vast array of diamonds, including rare and unique stones, directly to their customers at wholesale prices.

Quality and customer service are at the forefront of Aura Diamonds' values. Each diamond they sell is carefully selected and meticulously cut to ensure maximum brilliance and beauty. In addition to their custom design offerings, they also provide comprehensive diamond education to help their customers make informed decisions. Whether you're seeking a classic round cut or a more unusual shape, Aura Diamonds strives to make your diamond buying experience satisfying and memorable.
