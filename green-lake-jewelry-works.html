Green Lake Jewelry Works

Green Lake Jewelry Works is a renowned jewelry store located in Seattle and Bellevue, Washington, with a focus on handcrafting diamond jewelry pieces. Known for their exceptional craftsmanship, they offer a broad range of services, including custom made engagement rings, wedding bands, and other fine jewelry. Built on the principles of transparency, artistry, and sustainability, Green Lake Jewelry Works believes in a hands-on approach, allowing customers to be involved in the creative process of their piece, which is crafted by their team of skilled artisans.

Green Lake Jewelry Works is also committed to ethical sourcing and environment-friendly practices. They procure their diamonds and gemstones only from responsible sources that adhere to strict labor, trade, and environmental standards. As a part of their green initiative, they employ recycled precious metals in their creations. Their distinctive blend of personalized service, exceptional craftsmanship, and ethical business practices makes Green Lake Jewelry Works a trusted choice for customers seeking high-quality diamond jewelry.
