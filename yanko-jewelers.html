Yanko Jewelers

Yanko Jewelers is a family-owned business based in Miami, Florida, known for its extensive collection of fine diamond jewelry. With over three decades of experience in the jewelry industry, the company prides itself on providing high-quality pieces and exceptional customer service. Yanko Jewelers specializes in offering an array of diamond jewelry, including rings, necklaces, bracelets, and custom pieces, ensuring every customer can find something that suits their unique style and preferences.

Apart from selling a variety of diamond jewelry, Yanko Jewelers also offers jewelry repair, watch repair, and custom design services. Their team of skilled jewelers and craftsmen are dedicated to helping clients restore and transform their cherished pieces. With their commitment to quality, integrity, and personal service, Yanko Jewelers has established a reputation as a trusted name in the Miami jewelry scene.
