MDC Diamonds NYC

MDC Diamonds NYC is a distinguished New York City-based jeweler recognized for their expertise in diamonds and fine jewelry. Established by Moishy Krasnianski, a diamond professional with over 35 years of experience, the company offers a vast collection of exquisite, handcrafted diamond jewelry. With a vast network of diamond wholesalers around the globe, they ensure clients have access to some of the world's best diamonds at competitive prices.

At MDC Diamonds NYC, customers can expect a personalized and professional service. They offer custom-made diamond engagement rings, wedding bands, earrings, and other fine jewelry, allowing clients to participate in the design process for a truly unique piece. From choosing the diamond's size and shape to the design of the setting, MDC Diamonds NYC is dedicated to providing an exceptional experience that makes every customer feel valued and satisfied.
