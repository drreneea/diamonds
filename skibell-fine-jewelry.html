Skibell Fine Jewelry

Skibell Fine Jewelry is a premier Dallas-based jewelry store specializing in selling exquisite diamond pieces. The business, established in 1984, is renowned for its commitment to craftsmanship, quality, and personalized service. With a keen eye for detail, the store offers a vast collection of diamond jewelry, including engagement rings, necklaces, bracelets, and earrings, each piece carefully curated to ensure it meets the highest standards of clarity, color, cut, and carat weight.

The store is operated by Charles Skibell, a third-generation jeweler, who brings decades of experience and knowledge in the field of diamonds. Skibell Fine Jewelry also offers a range of services, such as jewelry repair, custom design, and gemstone evaluation, all delivered with the same dedication to client satisfaction. Whether you're seeking an engagement ring or a timeless piece to add to your collection, Skibell Fine Jewelry ensures a personalized and memorable shopping experience.
