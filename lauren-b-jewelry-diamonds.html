Lauren B Jewelry & Diamonds

Lauren B Jewelry & Diamonds is a family-owned business located in New York City's famed Diamond District. The company has a rich history dating back to the 1970s and is renowned for its exceptional craftsmanship and personalized service. They specialize in diamond engagement rings and wedding bands, offering a wide range of designs that cater to various tastes and budgets.

What sets Lauren B Jewelry & Diamonds apart is their commitment to customer satisfaction. They provide a comprehensive service that includes custom design, allowing customers to create their unique dream rings. Additionally, they offer jewelry cleaning and maintenance services to ensure that the pieces remain in pristine condition. With a vast selection of GIA and EGL certified diamonds, Lauren B Jewelry & Diamonds is dedicated to providing its clients with high-quality products that stand the test of time.
