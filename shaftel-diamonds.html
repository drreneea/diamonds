Shaftel Diamonds

Shaftel Diamonds, based in Houston, Texas, is a trusted name in the diamond and fine jewelry industry. With a history that dates back to the 1970s, this family-owned and operated business has been providing clients with high-quality diamonds and exceptional service for decades. The company prides itself on offering an extensive array of handpicked diamonds, including loose diamonds, engagement rings, and fine jewelry pieces.

The team at Shaftel Diamonds is renowned for its expertise and personalized service. They are committed to educating their clients about diamonds and helping them make informed decisions. Each diamond at Shaftel is selected with attention to quality and detail, ensuring that customers receive a piece of jewelry that is not only beautiful but also a good investment. Whether you're looking for a classic round cut or a unique custom design, Shaftel Diamonds has the knowledge and selection to help you find the perfect piece.
