Jean Dousset

Jean Dousset is a renowned high-end jewelry store that focuses primarily on diamond jewelry. Named after its Parisian founder, who is a descendant of the famous French jeweler Louis Cartier, the company reflects a rich heritage of exceptional craftsmanship and unique design. Dousset's exquisite attention to detail and commitment to perfection are evident in every piece of jewelry, which are all handcrafted in their Los Angeles workshop.

The company offers an impressive selection of engagement rings, wedding bands, necklaces, earrings, and other diamond accessories. Jean Dousset stands out for its bespoke service, where clients can collaborate directly with the design team to create a truly personalized piece. Additionally, they provide a seamless and informative online shopping experience, which includes 3D renderings of each jewelry design, ensuring customers can make a confident purchase.
