Harold Steven's Jewelers

Founded in 1980, Harold Steven's Jewelers is a San Diego-based, family-owned jewelry store specializing in diamond jewelry. The store offers customers a selection of unique, beautifully crafted pieces, from engagement rings and wedding bands to earrings and necklaces. Dedicated to high-quality craftsmanship and personal service, Harold Steven's Jewelers also provides custom jewelry design services, allowing customers to create their own unique pieces.

The store is well-known for its comprehensive selection of diamonds, each carefully chosen for its quality and brilliance. What sets Harold Steven's Jewelers apart is their commitment to ethical sourcing. They handpick their diamonds, ensuring they are conflict-free and adhere to the strictest labor and environmental standards. With an experienced team of jewelry professionals, Harold Steven's Jewelers offers expert guidance, helping customers find the perfect diamond piece that suits their style and budget.
