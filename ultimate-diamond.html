Ultimate Diamond

Ultimate Diamond is a well-established New York City-based retailer renowned for their high-end diamond jewelry. Founded in 1959, their long-standing reputation is a testament to their commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. They offer a comprehensive selection of diamond jewelry, including engagement rings, wedding bands, and fashion pieces.

With a team of experienced jewelers, Ultimate Diamond is dedicated to delivering exceptional craftsmanship and personalized service. They diligently source diamonds of superior quality, focusing on the four Cs (Cut, Clarity, Carat, and Color) to ensure every piece of jewelry meets the highest standards. Furthermore, they offer their customers the opportunity to create bespoke pieces, giving them a unique shopping experience.
