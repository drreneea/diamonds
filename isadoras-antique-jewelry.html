Isadoras Antique Jewelry

Isadoras Antique Jewelry is a reputable jewelry store located in Seattle, Washington, specializing in fine antique and vintage pieces, with a particular emphasis on diamond jewelry. With over 40 years of experience, they have curated a collection of unique and beautiful items, ranging from diamond engagement rings and wedding bands to necklaces, earrings, and bracelets. Their passion for history and romance is reflected in their selection of jewelry, which spans various eras including Victorian, Edwardian, Art Deco, and Mid-Century.

Isadoras is committed to offering exceptional customer service, providing expert advice and guidance to help customers find the perfect piece of jewelry. Each item in their collection is hand-selected for its beauty and craftsmanship, ensuring customers receive only the highest quality pieces. In addition to their retail store, Isadoras also operates an online shop, making their unique collection accessible to customers around the world. They also offer layaway and financing options, making it easier for customers to purchase their dream jewelry.
