Wedding Bands & Co.

Wedding Bands & Co. is a reputable jewelry store located in the heart of Chicago's Diamond District. With over 35 years of experience in the industry, the company takes pride in its mastery of the art and science of diamond selection, providing customers with an extensive range of high-quality diamond jewelry. Their skilled team of craftsmen and GIA-certified gemologists work diligently to ensure every piece is of the highest caliber, and is uniquely tailored to meet individual customer needs.

Wedding Bands & Co. is renowned for its specialization in engagement rings and wedding bands, but their expertise extends beyond these categories to include a variety of diamond jewelry. They maintain a customer-focused approach, offering a comfortable, private environment for clients to explore their extensive collection. The company is committed to ensuring each customer leaves with a piece that not only meets, but surpasses their expectations, making every occasion a memorable one.
