Shapiro Diamonds

Shapiro Diamonds is a Dallas-based bespoke jewelry boutique that offers a wide selection of high-quality diamond pieces. Established by Lance Shapiro and Kendra Shapiro, the company focuses on providing beautiful, unique and personalized diamond jewelry. With a commitment to craftsmanship and quality, they offer a range of diamonds and custom-made pieces, ensuring each customer finds a piece that reflects their personal style and taste.

With an emphasis on personal service, Shapiro Diamonds provides a private, one-on-one consultation to understand the customers' needs and preferences. They source their diamonds globally from reputable suppliers, ensuring each gemstone meets high standards for color, cut, clarity, and carat weight. Their collection includes engagement rings, wedding bands, earrings, bracelets, and other fine diamond jewelry, all designed and crafted with meticulous attention to detail.
