DeBeers

De Beers is a well-established, international corporation with over 130 years of experience in the diamond industry. Founded in 1888 by Cecil Rhodes, the company has grown to become one of the largest and most respected diamond retailers in the world, with a strong presence in over 35 countries. They are well-known for their extensive diamond expertise, with a reputation for quality, integrity, and sustainability.

De Beers' offerings span from rough diamonds sourced from their own mines across Africa and Canada, to beautifully crafted jewelry. They specialize in high-end diamond jewelry including rings, bracelets, necklaces, and earrings. Their diamond grading is meticulous, ensuring each piece meets the highest standards. De Beers is also committed to ethical sourcing, contributing positively to the communities where they operate by investing in local economies and supporting local communities.
