D&R House Of Diamonds

D&R House of Diamonds, based in Las Vegas, Nevada, is a private, appointment-based jewelry store specializing in high-quality diamonds. This exclusive boutique serves both domestic and international clientele with a keen focus on providing personalized service and creating custom jewelry designs. Established by Dave Padgett and Rich Classi, both industry veterans with over 50 years of combined experience, the store's mission is to offer superior products with competitive pricing and unparalleled service.

In addition to selling exquisite diamonds, D&R House of Diamonds also offers services such as jewelry repair, appraisal, and diamond education. They take pride in guiding clients through the diamond selection process, ensuring they find the perfect stone to match their style and budget. Their commitment to ethical sourcing, transparency, and customer satisfaction has built a significant reputation, making D&R House of Diamonds a trusted destination for diamond jewelry purchases.
