Padis Jewelry

Padis Jewelry is a trusted name in the diamond industry, operating in the heart of San Francisco since 1974. Their expert staff, many of whom are certified gemologists, meticulously select each diamond for its superior cut, color, and clarity. With a vast array of certified loose diamonds and exquisite engagement rings, Padis Jewelry is a one-stop-shop for customers seeking high-quality, ethically sourced diamonds.

Their personalized service extends beyond diamond sales. Padis provides jewelry design and repair via their team of skilled artisans. From custom designs to jewelry restorations, they ensure that every piece entrusted to them is handled with utmost care. Padis Jewelry prides itself in providing a warm, welcoming environment for all, making the diamond-buying process enjoyable and stress-free.
