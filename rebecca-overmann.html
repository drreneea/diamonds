Rebecca Overmann

Rebecca Overmann is a San Francisco-based jewelry brand known for its distinctive take on diamond jewelry. The brand was founded by Rebecca Overmann herself, a highly skilled artisan with a profound passion for creating beautiful pieces. The store offers a wide range of pieces, from engagement rings to earrings to bracelets, all of which are hand-forged using a mix of raw and refined diamonds. The brand's mission is to create jewelry that is not only beautiful but also meaningful, reflecting the unique stories and personalities of its wearers.

Overmann's design aesthetic is characterized by a naturalistic approach, focusing on organic shapes and textured surfaces. Each piece is meticulously crafted in the brand's studio, where traditional goldsmithing techniques meet modern design ideas. The result is a collection of timeless, wearable pieces that balance simplicity with an unexpected twist. Despite the brand's growth, Rebecca Overmann remains committed to sustainability and ethical sourcing, ensuring all diamonds are conflict-free and metals are recycled or responsibly mined.
