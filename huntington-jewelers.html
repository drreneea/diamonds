Huntington Jewelers

Huntington Jewelers is a well-established jewelry store, operating in Oklahoma City and Las Vegas for over 60 years. They specialize in the sale of high-quality diamond jewelry, with a particular focus on engagement rings and wedding bands. Their team of highly trained professionals offers exceptional customer service, guiding customers through their wide range of products to ensure they make the perfect choice.

In addition to their sales, Huntington Jewelers also offers custom design services for those seeking a unique piece of jewelry. Their in-house team of skilled designers and craftsmen use their expertise to bring your vision to life, whether you're redesigning an existing piece or creating something entirely new. Their commitment to quality and customer satisfaction makes them a reliable choice for any diamond jewelry needs.
