Ivy and Rose

Ivy & Rose is a Chicago-based bespoke jewelry store known for its specialization in diamond jewelry. With an emphasis on exquisite craftsmanship, the brand showcases a wide range of unique and elegant designs, from vintage-inspired pieces to modern styles. The company prides itself on its commitment to transparency and honesty, offering ethically sourced diamonds and other precious gemstones.

In addition to ready-to-wear pieces, Ivy & Rose offers custom design services, ensuring that every piece is as unique as the individual wearing it. Their team of experienced jewelers work closely with customers to bring their vision to life, whether it's an engagement ring, a wedding band, or a sentimental keepsake. Ivy & Rose is a destination for those seeking high-quality diamond jewelry with a personal touch.
