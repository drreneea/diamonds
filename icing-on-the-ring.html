Icing On The Ring

Icing On The Ring is a family-owned jewelry store located in the heart of Los Angeles' historic Jewelry District. Established in 1969, the store has been serving customers for over five decades, offering a wide range of diamond jewelry. They specialize in engagement rings and wedding bands, but also provide a beautiful selection of fashion jewelry, all crafted with meticulous attention to detail.

With a reputation built on trust and quality, Icing On The Ring takes pride in offering a personalized shopping experience. Their dedicated team of experts guides customers through the process of selecting the perfect piece of jewelry, providing education about diamonds and precious metals. Moreover, they offer custom design services for those seeking a truly unique piece. Their commitment to customer satisfaction is reflected in their high-quality jewelry and exceptional service.
