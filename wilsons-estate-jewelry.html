Wilson's Estate Jewelry

Wilson's Estate Jewelry is a family-owned business with a rich history of dealing in fine vintage, antique, and estate jewelry, with a particular emphasis on diamonds. Serving customers for over 50 years, the store has grown to become a trusted destination for those seeking high-quality and unique pieces. Their collection includes a wide range of jewelry from different periods, including Victorian, Edwardian, Art Deco, and more, each piece carrying its own story of craftsmanship and elegance.

The team at Wilson's Estate Jewelry is dedicated to providing excellent customer service, guiding patrons through their extensive inventory to help them find the perfect piece. Their expertise lies in identifying and appreciating the value of each piece, ensuring that customers receive genuine, valuable, and beautiful jewelry. Based in Montclair, New Jersey, their services extend online, offering worldwide shipping. Wilson's Estate Jewelry combines a deep knowledge of historical jewelry with a commitment to customer satisfaction, making it a reliable choice for anyone in search of timeless diamond pieces.
