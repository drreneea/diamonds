T-Bird Jewels

T-Bird Jewels, based in Las Vegas, is a family-owned business that specializes in the sale of high-quality diamonds and custom jewelry. With over 50 years of experience in the industry, they are known for their commitment to craftsmanship and their personal approach to customer service. The founder, Steven Michaels, is a seasoned gemologist who takes pride in educating customers, ensuring they make informed decisions about their purchases.

T-Bird Jewels offers an extensive range of diamonds, from loose stones to beautifully crafted engagement rings and fine jewelry. They also provide custom design services, allowing customers to collaborate with their in-house designers to create unique, personalized pieces. With an emphasis on quality and customer satisfaction, T-Bird Jewels has developed a reputation for its exceptional range of exquisite diamonds and fine jewelry.
