D'Errico Jewelry

D'Errico Jewelry is a family-owned business known for its expertise in diamonds and fine jewelry. Established in 1988 by two brothers, Richard and Michael D'Errico, the store has been serving its customers in Scarsdale, New York with a commitment to quality, integrity, and personal service. Over the years, they have established a reputation for crafting exquisite pieces and providing high-quality diamonds, sourced ethically and responsibly.

At D'Errico Jewelry, customer satisfaction is a priority. Their team of experienced jewelers and gemologists guide customers through the process of selecting the perfect diamond, ensuring a personalized and enjoyable shopping experience. The store offers a comprehensive range of services, including custom designs, jewelry repairs, and appraisals. Whether you're looking for engagement rings, wedding bands, or any other diamond jewelry, D'Errico Jewelry offers a blend of classic and contemporary styles to suit all tastes.
