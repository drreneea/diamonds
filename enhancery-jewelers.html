Enhancery Jewelers

Enhancery Jewelers, based in San Diego, California, is a family-owned and operated business that has been offering a wide variety of diamond jewelry for over 40 years. With an emphasis on quality, the company prides itself on providing its customers with a vast selection of certified diamonds and finely crafted jewelry, all at competitive prices. Their expert staff is well-equipped to guide customers through the process of choosing the perfect piece, whether it be for an engagement, anniversary, or another special occasion.

The company also offers custom design services, allowing customers to create unique and personal pieces of jewelry. Their experienced jewelers are skilled in transforming clients' visions into tangible works of art. In addition to sales, Enhancery Jewelers provides jewelry and watch repair services, ensuring that customers' cherished pieces can be maintained and enjoyed for years to come.
