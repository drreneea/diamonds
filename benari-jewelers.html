Benari Jewelers

Benari Jewelers is a family-owned business with over 60 years of experience in the industry, offering a wide selection of high-quality diamond jewelry. With two stores located in Exton and Newtown Square, Pennsylvania, they have built a reputation as a trusted source for engagement rings, wedding bands, fine jewelry, and watches. Their expert team provides personalized service to help customers find the perfect piece of jewelry to celebrate significant moments in their lives.

At Benari Jewelers, they believe in providing not just exquisite diamond jewelry, but also a shopping experience that is comfortable and enjoyable. They offer a variety of diamonds in different shapes and sizes, along with settings to suit individual preferences. They provide in-house financing, jewelry repair and maintenance, and even custom design services. Their dedication to customer satisfaction has earned them a loyal customer base and recognition in the industry.
