J.R. Dunn Jewelers

J.R. Dunn Jewelers is an established family-owned business that has been providing diamond jewelry to customers for over 50 years. The company prides itself on its commitment to quality and service, offering a wide range of pieces from engagement rings to fashion jewelry. Each piece is meticulously crafted, reflecting their dedication to offer only the finest jewelry in order to meet the diverse requirements of their clients.

Located in Lighthouse Point, Florida, J.R. Dunn Jewelers has grown to become one of the largest independent jewelers in the nation. The company's team of experienced jewelers and customer service representatives strive to provide a personalized shopping experience for every customer. Whether you are shopping in-store or online, J.R. Dunn Jewelers ensures a seamless and satisfying process from selection to purchase.
