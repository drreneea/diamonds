Ada Diamonds

Ada Diamonds is a fine jeweler that offers high-quality, lab-grown diamonds. Founded in 2015 by Lindsay Reinsmith and Jason Payne, the company is based in San Francisco, California, and operates primarily online, though they do have a showroom in New York City. Ada Diamonds specializes in designing and creating custom diamond jewelry, with a focus on ethical and sustainable practices.

The company's unique selling point is their lab-grown diamonds, which they argue are a more ethical choice than mined diamonds due to the reduced environmental impact and lack of involvement in conflict. Ada Diamonds offers a range of products, including engagement rings, wedding bands, and other fine jewelry. They pride themselves on their bespoke design service, which allows customers to create one-of-a-kind pieces tailored to their individual tastes and budgets.
